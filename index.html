About Me

I am an MSCS student at the University of Southern California, driven by a passion for software development, machine learning, and deep learning. My professional experience includes roles as a Software Development Intern at Blue Yonder, a Data Analytics Consultant at Thorogood Associates, and a Volunteer at Anna University Library. I specialize in implementing machine learning algorithms, developing deep learning models, and working with frameworks like Spring Boot, Node.js, React.js, and Angular. My technical proficiency spans multiple programming languages, including C++, Python, Java, and C#.

I hold an Integrated Master's in Computer Science from Anna University-CEG, where I graduated as the 2nd rank holder in my class with a CGPA of 8.98/10. Beyond academics, I have demonstrated a strong commitment to community engagement through leadership roles such as Director of the Computer Society of Anna University, Treasurer of the National Service Scheme, Workshop Organizer at CEG Tech Forum, Organizer of CEG Reads, and Student Representative. Through these roles, I have led impactful initiatives, volunteer programs, and workshops, seamlessly combining my technical expertise with efforts to create meaningful social impact.
